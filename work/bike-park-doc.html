Building a Dirt Jump Oasis for the Public

Status: Released | Watch Now Available

A year-long project following the creation of a bike park near my hometown. What started as documenting some jumps being built became something much bigger - a story about a struggle, a mans vision, and what it takes to build something from nothing on public land.

Deven Schneider had been obsessing over dirt for decades. The city of San Marcos gave him a flat piece of ground and said 'build something.' This is what happened.
